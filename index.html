What is Good Game Con!?

A focused event built around real product discovery and real community energy — not just looking at stuff behind glass.

We’re bringing portable audio, gaming peripherals, and anime culture into one space — designed for trying, comparing, and connecting.

  • Hands-on first: Try products properly before you buy.
  • Community-built: Meet creators, hobbyists, and new friends.
  • Brand-friendly: A showroom approach that helps people discover fit and value.
